About Me

Background, skills, and how I approach software development.

Background

I’m a Computer Programming graduate from Georgian College, based in Toronto, originally from Brazil. Moving to a new country and building a career in tech has made me adaptable, curious, and committed to continuous learning.

I enjoy building practical software and learning by shipping real projects. My main focus is backend development, APIs, and cloud platforms, with hands-on experience using AWS and growing familiarity with Firebase and Azure.

How I Work
